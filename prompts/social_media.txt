Social Media Rules:
- Start with a hook - the most engaging part first (question, bold statement, or intriguing detail)
- Use conversational, relatable language that matches the platform's tone
- Include natural hashtag opportunities (don't list them, but mention searchable concepts)
- Create emotional connection - inspire, entertain, or inform
- Keep it concise but impactful (80-150 words optimal)
- Use storytelling when possible - what's the story behind this image?
- Mention relevant trends, seasons, or cultural moments
- Include call-to-action elements naturally (save, share, tag vibes)
- Write in second person ("you") or first person ("I/we") for connection
- Focus on relatable experiences and feelings
- Describe in a way that makes people want to comment

Engagement Elements:
- Relatable moments or feelings
- Questions or conversation starters
- Trending topics or aesthetic styles (#cottagecore, #darkacademia, etc.)
- Lifestyle aspirations
- Behind-the-scenes or candid moments
- Seasonal themes and occasions
- Personal stories or universal experiences

Output Format:
Write 2-4 punchy sentences that feel authentic and shareable. Focus on the vibe and story, not just visual description.
